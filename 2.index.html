Travel to Maasai Mara Game Reserve.

The Maasai Mara Game Reserve is located a few kilometres from Narok County. It is one of the seven wonders of the world. It is mostly famous for the wildbeast that cross the Mara River.

Wild animals present in the park.

The game reserve contains different species of wild animals and birds. The big five are also present in the park. This include;
  1. Lions.
  2. Buffaloes.
  3. Elephant.
  4. Rhino.
  5. Leopard.

Activities to engage in.

There are various activities that the tourist is able to participate in as they enjoy an amazing experience at the game reserve. This activities include:

  • Bird watching.
  • Game drives.
  • Bush dinners.
  • Flying in hot air baloons.
  • Maasai dances.

Maasai Mara Game Reserve is adjacent to Serengeti National Park and the two are separated by the Mara River. You are also able to have a beautiful view of Mt. Kilimanjaro incase you are flying from Nairobi to the game reserve.

The Maasai Mara Game Reserve has high standards of hospitality and all are welcome to visit.
