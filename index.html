Recipe #1- Ugali

  • Time: 1:00 p.m.
  • Serves: 1:45 p.m.
Ingredients Quantity
maize flour 1 cup
water 3 cups

Step 1: Boil water until it bubbles. The water should be very hot. This ensures the ugali will cook perfectly.
Step 2: Add a cup full of flour into the water. Let it cook a few seconds until water starts to cover the flour.
Step 3: Use a wooden spoon to quickly start mixing the flour and water.
Step 4: Add a handful of flour and continue to mix the water and flour it will start to come together.
Step 5: Reduce heat to medium and keep turning the ugali as it continues to stick together.
Step 6: Gather the ugali and press it onto the side of the sufuria.
Step 7: Place the wooden spoon under the ugali and form into a ball in the middle of the sufuria.
Step 8: Repeat pressing the ugali on the side of the sufuria and turning it to the middle.
Step 9: Once the ugali has become firm, turn it once more and smoothen it into a round. You will know your ugali is ready once you start to smell the aroma clearly.
Step 10: Turn the ugali over onto a plate.
Step 11: Dip the wooden spoon into water and use it to smoothen the ugali into a round ball on the plate.
Step 12: Serve the ugali whole or slice. Enjoy with your favorite stew, meat, veggies, fish etc.

Recipe #2- Mahamri

  • Time: 2:00 p.m.
  • Serves: 3:00 p.m.
Ingredients Quantity
wheat flour 2 cups
margarine 3 table spoon
baking powder 1 teaspoon
sugar 3 tablespoon
salt a pinch
milk 1/2 a cup
cooking oil enough to fry all dough prepared
lemon rinds 3 tablespoons

Step 1: Melt the margarine and add in the flour,add in the baking powder,sugar,salt,lemon rind and mix.
Step 2: Add in the milk in bits and knead until the dough is soft and doesn't stick on hands.
Step 3: Cover for about 45 minutes with a damp cloth.
Step 4: Roll out the dough on a flat surface not very thick. Cut into desired shapes and deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.
Step 5: Serve. Enjoy with any beverage ie. tea, coffee, juice, porridge etc.

Recipe #3- Beef Samosa

  • Time: 3:00 p.m.
  • Serves: 4:00 p.m.
Ingredients Quantity
wheat flour 2 cups
minced beef 1/2 kg
salt a pinch
dhania bunch
onion 1
cooking oil enough to fry all the dough

Step 1: Knead dough until soft. Leave to rest 15 minutes. Divide dough into small balls. Roll one of the balls into a round shape as thinly as possible. Place dough on pan, cook 1 minute slightly. Cook all dough, set aside. Mix the minced meat with dhania and onion and fry, it should not be wet.
Step 2: Slice the round dough into half and fold the samosa pockets into triangles. Stuff the minced beef into the pockets. Fill all pockets.
Step 3: Heat the oil until hot. Add the samosa pockets, start frying.
Step 4: Fry samosas until golden brown.
Step 5: Crispy delicious beef samosas.
Step 6: Serve immediately.
