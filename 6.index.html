Travel to Lake Nakuru National Park

Lake Nakuru National Park is located in Nakuru County. The National Park is adjacent to the salty Lake Nakuru. The park is home to a variety of different animals and bird species. The big five are all present within the park and therefore one is looking to have an amazing experience during a visit. These animals include:

  • Lion
  • Buffalo
  • Rhino
  • Elephant
  • Leopard

Both local and international tourists flock in to visit this magical place. Five star hotels are situated in and around the park ie. Serena, Sarova and Kivu Hotels.

The price charges are fair to accomodate all those interested in visiting the National Park. Lake Nakuru is home to a large number of beautiful flamingoes that migrate to and from Lake Bogoria. Some of the fun activities tourists can engage in are:

  1. Game drives
  2. Bird watching
  3. Bush dinners

All are welcome to visit and have a wonderful time at this beautiful scenery Nakuru County has to offer.
